# 🍽 Swiggy Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Swiggy food delivery data to uncover insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and order trends. The dashboard helps identify key revenue drivers and business patterns.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze sales performance across cities and time periods  
- Understand customer preferences (Veg vs Non-Veg)  
- Identify peak order days and high-performing locations  
- Provide actionable insights for business decision-making  

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables  
- Data Cleaning  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- 💰 Generated **₹53.01M total revenue** from **197K+ orders**  
- 📊 Maintained strong **average rating of 4.34** across **5.59M ratings**  
- 🥗 **Veg orders contributed 66%** of total revenue vs 34% Non-Veg  
- 📅 **Saturday & Thursday** identified as peak revenue days  
- 📆 Monthly sales remained stable between **₹6.2M – ₹6.8M**  
- 🏆 **Q2 emerged as top-performing quarter** (₹19.9M revenue)  
- 🌆 **Bengaluru** was the highest revenue-generating city (₹5.5M)  


## 💡 Business Impact
This analysis helps businesses:
- Optimize marketing strategies based on peak days  
- Focus on high-performing cities for expansion  
- Improve product offerings based on customer preferences  
- Enhance decision-making using data-driven insights
